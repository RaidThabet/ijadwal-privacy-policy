# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 01/02/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **IJadwal** (“the App”) handles user information.

## 1. Data Collection
**IJadwal does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data or usage data.**  
The App does not require users to create an account, provide personal information, or grant access to sensitive device features.

## 2. Advertising
The App **does not display ads** and **does not use any advertising SDKs** or third-party tracking technologies.

## 3. Analytics and Tracking
The App **does not use analytics tools**, cookies, tracking pixels, or similar technologies.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App does **not integrate with third-party services** that collect, process, or share user data.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
The App is intended for users aged **16 and above**.  
However, children may technically be able to access the App. Since the App does not collect personal data, include communication features, or display advertisements, **no personal information from children is collected, knowingly or unknowingly**.

The App has no functionality that allows users to submit personal information.

## 6. Data Security
Because the App does not collect or store any user data, there is **no risk of personal data exposure** related to its use.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this document.
